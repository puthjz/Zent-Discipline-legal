## Frequently Asked Questions

### Account & Data

**How do I delete my account?**
Go to **Settings → Delete Account**. This permanently deletes all your data — goals, progress logs, wallet records, and profile information. This action is irreversible.

**Is my data synced across devices?**
Yes. Your data is stored in the cloud and syncs automatically across all devices where you're signed in to the same Zent account.

**How do I sign in?**
Zent uses Google or Apple Sign-In for authentication. Tap **Continue with Google** or **Continue with Apple** on the login screen.

---

### Goals & Streaks

**How do I add a new goal?**
Tap the **+** button on the Home screen and choose a goal type — Check, Timer, Progress, Range, Journal, or Expense.

**How do streaks work?**
Your streak increases by 1 each day you complete a goal. If you miss a day, the streak resets to 0. Streaks reset at midnight in your local time zone.

**Can I edit or delete a goal?**
Yes. Open the goal, tap the settings or edit icon, and choose Edit or Delete.

---

### Subscription

**What is Zent Premium?**
Zent Premium unlocks unlimited goals, advanced analytics, and PDF export. It is available as a monthly, annual, or lifetime plan.

**How do I upgrade to Premium?**
Go to **Settings → Zent Premium** and choose a plan.

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
Subscriptions are managed by Apple. To cancel:
1. Open **Settings** on your iPhone.
2. Tap your name → **Subscriptions**.
3. Find **Zent: Discipline** and tap **Cancel Subscription**.

Cancelling stops future renewals. You retain access until the end of your current billing period.

**How do I restore a previous purchase?**
Go to **Settings → Zent Premium → Restore Purchases**.

---

### Notifications

**How do I enable daily reminders?**
Go to **Settings** in the app and toggle on **Daily Tracking Notification**. You'll receive reminders at 8 AM, 12 PM, and 6 PM.

**How do I turn off notifications?**
Toggle off **Daily Tracking Notification** in **Settings**, or disable notifications for Zent in your iPhone's system **Settings → Notifications → Zent: Discipline**.

---

### Ads

**Why do I see ads?**
Ads are shown to users on the free plan. Upgrade to **Zent Premium** to remove all ads.
