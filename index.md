# Get 1% Better Every Day.

**Zent: Discipline** is the habit and goal tracking app for people serious about building daily discipline. Set goals, track your progress, build unbroken streaks — and watch your life change one day at a time.

The name *Zent* comes from *one percent* — the compounding power of small, consistent daily improvement.

## What You Can Track

Zent supports every kind of goal you want to build discipline around:

| Goal Type | What It Does |
|---|---|
| **Check** | Simple yes/no daily habits (e.g. "Did I work out today?") |
| **Timer** | Time-based goals with a built-in stopwatch (e.g. "Read for 30 minutes") |
| **Progress** | Numeric progress toward a daily target (e.g. "Drink 8 glasses of water") |
| **Range** | Stay within a target range (e.g. "Sleep 7–9 hours") |
| **Journal** | Daily text reflection and journaling |
| **Expense** | Daily spending tracking against a budget goal |

---

## Features

### Streak Tracking
Every day you complete your goal, your streak grows. Miss a day and it resets. Simple. Honest. Motivating.

### Progress Analytics
See your completion rate, longest streak, and progress history at a glance. Know exactly where you stand.

### Daily Reminders
Get three optional daily push notifications — morning, midday, and evening — so your goals stay top of mind.

### Zent Premium
Unlock unlimited goals, advanced stats, and PDF exports with **Zent Premium**. Available as monthly, annual, and lifetime plans.

### Your Data, Your Privacy
All your data is stored securely. You can export it or delete it at any time from within the app.
